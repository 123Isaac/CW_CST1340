About US

Welcome to the Library of Ruina, where every book holds a story waiting to be uncovered. We are more than just a bookstore—we are a gateway to worlds unknown, filled with knowledge, mystery, and imagination. Our collection is carefully chosen to bring together powerful stories, hidden truths, and unforgettable journeys. Step inside, and let the pages guide you to places you’ve never been before. Library of Ruina is located in the vibrant winding streets of Port Louis, just steps away from the historic harbor. Library of Ruina is more than just a bookstore—it is a gateway. We are a collective of local bibliophiles and archivists who believe that every book found in the 'ruins' of time deserves a second life. In a city that never stops moving, we offer a quiet, neon-lit refuge for the dreamers and the scholars. From rare Mauritian historical manuscripts to the latest global fiction, our shelves are curated to inspire the curious soul. Whether you are a student from the University or a traveler passing through the docks, we invite you to step out of the tropical sun and into our world. At Library of Ruina, we don't just sell books; we preserve the stories that define us.

Our Collection

At Library of Ruina, we pride ourselves on our diverse and carefully curated collection of books. Our shelves are filled with a wide range of genres, including fiction, non-fiction, history, science, art, and more. We believe that every reader deserves to find something that resonates with them, which is why we strive to offer a variety of titles that cater to different tastes and interests. Whether you're looking for the latest bestseller, a classic novel, or a rare find, our collection has something for everyone. We also feature works by local Mauritian authors, showcasing the rich literary talent of our island. Our knowledgeable staff is always on hand to help you discover new books and authors that you might not have encountered before.

Join Our Story

We invite you to become part of the Library of Ruina community. Whether you're a frequent visitor or a new face, we welcome you to explore our collection, participate in our events, and contribute to the stories that make our library unique.

Why Us

At Library of Ruina, we are committed to providing a unique and enriching experience for all our patrons. Our dedicated staff is passionate about literature and eager to help you discover new favorites or revisit beloved classics.
