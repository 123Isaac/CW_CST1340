About Us

Welcome to the Library of Ruina, where every book holds a story waiting to be uncovered. We are more than just a bookstore—we are a gateway to worlds unknown, filled with knowledge, mystery, and imagination. Our collection is carefully chosen to bring together powerful stories, hidden truths, and unforgettable journeys. Step inside, and let the pages guide you to places you’ve never been before. library of Ruina is Located in the vibrant winding streets of Port Louis, just steps away from the historic harbor, Library of Ruina is more than just a bookstore—it is a gateway. We are a collective of local bibliophiles and archivists who believe that every book found in the 'ruins' of time deserves a second life. In a city that never stops moving, we offer a quiet, neon-lit refuge for the dreamers and the scholars. From rare Mauritian historical manuscripts to the latest global fiction, our shelves are curated to inspire the curious soul. Whether you are a student from the University or a traveler passing through the docks, we invite you to step out of the tropical sun and into our world. At Library of Ruina, we don't just sell books; we preserve the stories that define us.
